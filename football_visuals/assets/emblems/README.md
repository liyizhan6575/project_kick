# Example emblems — illustrative use only

The image(s) here are **example tournament / competition emblems**, recoloured to white with a
transparent background, used to demonstrate the `emblem` slot of the motion canvas
([`football_visuals/animations/canvas.py`](../../animations/canvas.py)).

**These marks are the trademarks of their respective owners.** They are included here **for
illustration and visualisation only** — this project is **not affiliated with, endorsed by, or
sponsored by** any league, competition, or governing body, and claims no ownership of the marks. They
carry no project licence; the underlying trademark rights remain entirely with their owners.

If you reuse this repository, supply your own emblem assets, or remove these — the canvas takes the
emblem as a parameter precisely so the library itself ships no third-party marks.
